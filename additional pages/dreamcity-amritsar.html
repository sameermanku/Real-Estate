DreamCity Amritsar

DreamCity, Amritsar, 148 acres of integrated township appreciates quality living, fine tastes and refined lifestyles.

Offering choices of villas, independent floors and plots, DreamCity's unique architecture and use of state-of-the-art technology have set an unparalleled benchmark of luxury and comfort. It also offers best of the best conveniences at your doorstep. And now with its successful launch in Canada, USA and UK, DreamCity is all set to provide an upscale living experience and a lucrative investment opportunity to NRIs back home.

AIPL Ambuja DreamCity, spread over 148 acres is located on the main Jalandhar-Amritsar Highway. Comprising of plots, independent villas and premium floors, this eco-friendly township is has been designed and mastered by internationally acclaimed Hafeez Contractor. Moreover, it is replete with a beautiful ambience and modern amenities like Club, Power Back up, Children play area etc. Rejuvenating yoga ground, swimming pool & toddler's pool, playing area for tennis, badminton, squash, table tennis, cards & chess, fine dining restaurant, bar, coffee shop and pastry kiosk, a trendy and spacious gaming zone with video game parlour, bowling alley & billiards/snooker.
