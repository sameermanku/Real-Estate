DreamCity Gobindgarh-Khanna

Away from the chaos and clutter of the city, DreamCity, Gobindgarh-Khanna is a complete integrated township planned along the lines of one of the best communities in the world.

Designed by Surbana, Singapore, its sound planning is reflected in beautiful layouts, green environs, clean water and air, while cutting edge technology assures efficient functioning of all systems. Designed to take the hardwork out of urban living, this is a town that works for you. Day after day, offering choices of villas, independent floors, and plots, DreamCity seeks to evoke and inspire the spirit of family living in a reserved yet privileged neighbourhood.

DreamCity is an expensive, lush green 88 acres landscaped respite that indulges you in a life of luxury. A Pinnacle of living, reminiscent of the grand Neo-Victorian architecture with compelling living spaces and all the spoils for a life of opulence. DreamCity offers a variety of luxurious villas, plots, independent floors, and a plethora of leisure and retail options.

Township Facilities and Infrastructure

  • Gated community
  • Proposed development for Hotel & World Class Medical care facilities, leisure, and retail options
  • 5km of wide planned roads supplemented with walk ways, to ensure hassle free access & circulation of traffic
  • Underground sewerage treatment plant
  • Underground electrical cable network system & more
  • Beautifully manicured central green on 2 acres, dedicated for the use and enjoyment of residents and their families
  • DreamCity Club - Swimming pool, Tennis, Squash, Badminton courts, Aerobics room with Yoga & multipurpose hall. Spa with Jaccuzi, steam & sauna and gymnasium. Carrom, Billiards, Table Tennis room, and many more
Products available

  • Plots: 350 sq yd onwards
  • Floors: Ferbdale Floor - 400 sq yd
  • Villas: Bradford Villa - 350 sq yd & Wallingford Villa - 500 sq yd
Why Gobindgarh - Khanna

  • Home to largest grain market in Asia and the hub of steel plants, the region is full of wealth and prosperity
  • The area located right next to Ludhiana, aptly called the "Manchester of India".
  • Gobindgarh & Khanna has close proximity to national highways and thus enjoys excellent connectivity.
